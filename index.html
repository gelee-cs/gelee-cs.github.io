About

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the School of Computing Technologies, RMIT University, Australia. I am supervised by Prof. Zhifeng Bao, Prof. Shane Culpepper and Dr. Shixun Huang.

My research explores the intersection of databases and machine learning, with a particular focus on data management and data preparation. I aim to develop practical, efficient, and cost-effective solutions that enhance the accessibility and quality of data for real-world applications.
